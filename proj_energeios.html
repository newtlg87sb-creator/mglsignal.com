What is Energeios?

Energeios is building a decentralized network of electric vehicle (EV) charging stations, powered by individuals and businesses. Our mission is to accelerate the adoption of sustainable transportation by making EV charging accessible and profitable for everyone.

By leveraging blockchain technology, we create a transparent and efficient ecosystem where charger owners (Hosts) are rewarded with our native token ($EVS) for providing energy, and EV drivers can easily find and pay for charging services through a simple mobile application.

The Problem

The world is shifting to electric vehicles, but the public charging infrastructure is lagging behind. This creates "range anxiety" for drivers and slows down EV adoption.

Our Solution

Energeios empowers anyone to become part of the solution. By installing an Energeios-compatible charger, you can provide a public service and earn passive income every time someone charges their vehicle.

Registration Guide

1

Use the "Register Now" button on our website to ensure you are using the correct referral link.

2

Fill in Your Details

Enter your email address, WhatsApp number, create a secure password, and confirm the referral code is filled in correctly.

3

Request OTP

Click the 'Send OTP' button. A One-Time Password will be sent to your email. Be sure to check your inbox and spam folder.

4

Verify and Login

Enter the OTP you received and click 'Create Account' to log in to your new Energeios dashboard.

Link Account Guide

  1. Go to the 'My Profile' section in the 'Profile Management' menu.
  2. Find the 'Edit Address' section on the page that opens.
  3. Paste your BEP20 wallet address into the address input field. (Only BEP20 addresses are supported)
  4. Check if you have entered the address correctly and click the 'Update Address' button to save.

Withdrawal Guide

  1. Navigate to the "Wallet" or "Dashboard" section in your Energeios account.
  2. Click on the "Withdraw" button next to your $EVS balance.
  3. Enter the amount you wish to withdraw and paste your BEP-20 wallet address.
  4. Confirm the transaction. Withdrawals are processed within 30 minute. A small network fee may apply.
